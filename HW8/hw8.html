Max Neuwirth

Research question:

What is the percentage of Washington D.C. affordable housing outside walking distance (.25 miles) of a metro station?

Method:

1. Created the .25 mile buffer of “walking distance” around each metro stop

2. Used the difference tool to get the homes outside the “walking distance” buffers

3. Manually counted the number of homes remaining inside the “walking distance” buffers

4. Subtracted from number of affordable housing units in the original layer attribute table to get number of homes outside the “walking distance” buffers

5. Divided by number of affordable housing units in the original layer attribute table to get percentage of affordable housing projects outside walking distance of a metro station

Findings:

77.6% of affordable housing in Washington D.C. is outside walking distance of a metro station
